About Estimator

Estimator is an application designed to estimate the cost of projects based on utilized technologies, man-hours, risk factors, and a variety of other parameters.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive project cost estimation based on user-provided parameters, ensuring accurate budgeting and planning.
  • In-depth consideration of risks and their impact on costs, enabling proactive management and mitigation strategies.
  • Flexible settings for various project types, allowing customization to meet the specific needs of diverse industries and project scopes.
  • Intuitive and user-friendly interface designed to streamline the estimation process, reducing time spent on training and increasing productivity.
  • Ability to save and export estimates in multiple formats (e.g., PDF, Excel), facilitating easy sharing and integration with other tools and stakeholders.
